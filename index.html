Contextual Information

Graphic design is everywhere, even when people don’t notice it. Every logo, sneaker box, concert poster, app layout, and social media ad was carefully designed to grab attention and communicate something instantly. What interests me most about graphic design is how creativity and technology work together to influence the way people see brands, products, and ideas every day.

For this project, I wanted to explore more than just popular applications like Photoshop or Illustrator. I wanted to look at the tools, hardware, and technology behind the creative process, from cameras and drawing tablets to digital design platforms. Graphic design is not only about making things look good — it is about solving problems visually and creating experiences people remember.
